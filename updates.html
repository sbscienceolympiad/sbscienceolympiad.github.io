Updates

Here are some of our recent competition results and other important announcements.

FEB 1 2025

Princeton University Invitational

Our team competed at the Princeton University Invitational competition placing 18th and 42nd overall out of 56 teams! Special congratulations to our Astronomy (3rd), Disease Detective (6th), Robot Tour (2nd) teams for placing!

JAN 25 2025

Columbia University Invitational

Our team competed at the Columbia University Invitational competition placing 14th and 29th overall out of 60 teams! Special congratulations to our Astronomy (5th), Dynamic Planet (5th), Helicopter (2nd), Material Science (2nd), Optics (3rd), and Picture This (3rd) teams for placing!

JAN 17 2025

Regionals

Our team competed at the UCNJ Regional competition placing 5th overall out of 23 teams! Special congratulations to our Air Trajectory (4th), Astronomy (6th), Dynamic Planet (6th), Entomology (5th), Helicopter (2nd), Microbe Mission (3rd), Optics (2nd), Tower (3rd), and Wind Power (6th) teams for placing!
